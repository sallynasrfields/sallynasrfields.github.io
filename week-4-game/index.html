Crystal Mystical Math Game

Instructions

This game is made up of:

  1. Four clickable crystals
  2. Your total number
  3. Your target number.

At the beginning of each game (1) one random number is generated as your target number & (2) four random numbers, unknown to you, are generated and assigned to each of the crystals. Your total starts at "0". Each time a crystal is clicked, its linked number is added to your total.

Your goal is to match your total to the target. If your total surpasses your target you lose.
